About Me

I am the founder of Takatanium LLC, a business and scientific consulting firm. My clients have consisted of start-ups in nanotechnology, biotechnology, robotics, and data analytics. I have a strong interest in utilizing new technologies to accelerate the progress of the physical sciences.


In 2010, I graduated from the Georgia Institute of Technology with a PhD in Quantum Computational Chemistry. I then worked as a Post-Doctoral Associate at Cornell University in Biotechnology researching adhesion properties of circulating tumor cells. Currently, my main specialties as a consultant are modeling chemical systems via quantum and molecular mechanics and developing various scientific software applications. I have experience in C, C++, HTML, CSS, JS, PHP, Python, and Perl.


On a less technical side, I worked as a ski and bicycle technician throughout college, where I was also the associate store manager. More recently, I was a personal trainer in San Diego, CA.
